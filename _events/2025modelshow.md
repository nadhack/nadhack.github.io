After the 2025 success of this event we will be running it again in 2026. As part of this year’s Arts Festival, we are hosting the Model Show in the Memorial Hall again, on the same day as the classic car show. 
We are expecting a large range of models to be displayed from individual model makers and local clubs. Items on display ranging from Lego, Meccano, Plastic kits, Model Railway, Live Steam and much more.
There will also be a demonstration on Warhammer modeling, so if you are looking at getting some tips on painting your figures, pop along and ask the experts.
